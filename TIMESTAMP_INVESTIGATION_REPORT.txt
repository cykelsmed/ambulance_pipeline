
================================================================================
COMPREHENSIVE TIMESTAMP ANALYSIS - ALL 5 REGIONS
================================================================================
Investigation Date: 2025-11-08
Purpose: Understand what "response time" measures and identify available timestamps

KEY FINDING: The official "ResponsMinutter" measures TOTAL PATIENT WAIT TIME
(from 112 call received until ambulance/first unit arrives at scene)
NOT just dispatch-to-arrival travel time.

This means the published response times INCLUDE:
1. Call handling time (112 operator to triage)
2. Triage/classification time (determining priority)
3. Dispatch time (assigning ambulance)
4. Travel time (ambulance en route to scene)
